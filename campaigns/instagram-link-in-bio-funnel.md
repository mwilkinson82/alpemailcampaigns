## Capture Form

Minimum fields:

- First name.
- Email.

Useful fields:

- Company name.
- Annual revenue range.
- Primary money leak.
- Trade / contractor type.
- Number of field employees.

Recommended annual revenue choices:

- Under $500K.
- $500K to $1M.
- $1M to $3M.
- $3M to $7M.
- $7M to $15M.
- $15M+.

Recommended primary money leak choices:

- Winning bad jobs.
- Doing extra work for free.
- Jobs look fine until they do not.
- I am still the bottleneck.
- My people do not own outcomes.
- I need systems but do not know where to start.

- Invite to Contractor Circle.

Project profit leak:

- Send master Profit Leak sequence.

Owner-dependence leak:

- Invite to Contractor Circle.

Accountability leak:

- Send master Profit Leak sequence.

- Explain that assignments, implementation paths, and call replays are member-only.
- Invite to Contractor Circle.
