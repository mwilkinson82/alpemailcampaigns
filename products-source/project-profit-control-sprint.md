# Product: Project Profit Control Sprint

## Product Role

This is one of two active low-ticket buyer filters.

It is the first IOR / OverWatch paid bridge.

It should identify buyers, not become the center of the business.

It should not be sold as "IOR" first.

It should be sold as:

```text
Get control of one live job and see what is happening to the money.
```

## One-Sentence Promise

```text
In 7 days, get control of one live job by identifying the profit leaks, putting dollars beside the risks, and creating an owner/PM action plan.
```

## Audience

Best fit:

- Small contractors with one or more active jobs.
- Owners with one PM or a lead guy managing jobs.
- Contractors who are busy but unsure which jobs are actually making money.
- Contractors with change-order drag, schedule drag, cash pressure, or weak project financial visibility.

## Outcome

By the end, the buyer has:

- One completed profit leak score.
- One job profit snapshot.
- Top-five risks with dollar impact.
- Change-order exposure review.
- Owner/PM weekly review agenda.
- 30-day project profit action plan.

## Modules

### Day 1: Pick The Job

Output:

- Job baseline, contract value, current expected profit, and open concerns.

### Day 2: Find The Leaks

Output:

- Contractor Profit Leak Audit score.

### Day 3: Put Dollars Beside Risk

Output:

- Top-five risk register with dollar impact.

### Day 4: Review Change Orders And Cash

Output:

- Open change-order and billing exposure view.

### Day 5: Build The Owner/PM View

Output:

- Shared financial view of risk, profit, and next actions.

### Day 6: Build The Weekly Job Profit Review

Output:

- Weekly project profit review agenda.

### Day 7: Decide The Next System

Output:

- 30-day plan and recommendation for IOR, AOS, or Contractor Circle.
