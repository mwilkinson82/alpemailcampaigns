# Product: Change Order Cash Kit

## Product Role

This is one of two active low-ticket buyer filters.

It is a paid bridge from simple money pain into process, ownership, IOR, and weekly control.

It should identify buyers, not become the center of the business.

## One-Sentence Promise

```text
Track change orders until they become approved, billed, and collected cash.
```

## Audience

Best fit:

- Contractors doing extra work before approval.
- Owners chasing change orders from memory.
- PMs who talk about changes but do not move them to cash.
- Contractors with cash pressure created by unbilled or uncollected work.

## Outcome

By the end, the buyer has:

- A visible change-order tracker.
- Weekly review process.
- Owner/PM accountability.
- Cash exposure view.
- Follow-up rhythm.

## Modules

### 1. Find The Open Money

Output:

- List of every open change, value, owner, and next move.

### 2. Track The Six Dates

Output:

- Identified, priced, submitted, approved, billed, and collected dates.

### 3. Calculate Cash Exposure

Output:

- Total unapproved, approved but unbilled, and billed but uncollected value.

### 4. Assign Ownership

Output:

- One accountable owner for each next move.

### 5. Weekly Change-Order Meeting

Output:

- Weekly rhythm that keeps change orders from disappearing into inboxes and conversations.
