# AOS Integration

AOS is the company operating system product.

It does not own public lead magnets or drip campaigns.

## AOS Owns

- AOS product experience.
- Company operating system tools.
- Roles.
- Scorecards.
- Meeting rhythms.
- Accountability workflows.

## Campaign Events To Send

- `aos_signup_started`
- `aos_trial_started`
- `aos_customer_created`
- `aos_activation_completed`

## Campaign Relationship

Public campaign assets can educate leads on owner dependence, accountability, roles, scorecards, and operating rhythm.

Full AOS tools stay gated inside AOS or Contractor Circle Hub.
