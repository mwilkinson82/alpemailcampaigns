# Immediate 14-Day Action Plan

Use this after the first-week sprint in:

- `operations/first-7-days.md`

The first mission is to make the Contractor Profit Leak Audit the center of the public funnel and the member reset before expanding the rest of the machine.

## Day 1: Define The Machine

Internal statement:

```text
Contractor Circle helps owner-led contractors stop leaking profit, get control of jobs, and build companies that do not depend on the owner carrying every decision.
```

Everything must point back to that.

## Day 2: Build The Profit Leak Audit Questions

Create 25 questions across five sections:

- Estimating.
- Change Orders.
- Project Profit Control.
- Owner Bottleneck.
- Accountability.

## Day 3: Build The Result Types

Create the five result pages:

- The Bad-Bid Contractor.
- The Free-Work Contractor.
- The Blind-Job Contractor.
- The Bottleneck Owner.
- The No-Accountability Contractor.

## Day 4: Build The Landing Page

Page structure:

```text
Headline:
Find where your construction company is leaking profit.

Subheadline:
Most contractors do not lose money in one big obvious place. They leak it through estimating, change orders, project management, owner bottlenecks, and lack of accountability.

CTA:
Take the free Contractor Profit Leak Audit.

Sections:
- What the audit checks
- Who it is for
- What you get
- Why profit leaks happen
- CTA again
```

## Day 5: Build The First Email Sequence

Seven emails:

- Email 1: Your Profit Leak result.
- Email 2: Why contractors leak profit even when busy.
- Email 3: The estimating leak.
- Email 4: The change-order leak.
- Email 5: The project profit leak.
- Email 6: The owner bottleneck leak.
- Email 7: Contractor Circle invitation.

## Day 6: Build Change Order Cash Tracker

Build the first concrete tool:

- Spreadsheet/tracker.
- Instructions.
- Example filled-out version.
- Weekly meeting agenda.

## Day 7: Create The Circle Reset Announcement

Send/post:

```text
Contractor Circle is moving into a new implementation model.

We are starting with the Contractor Profit Leak Audit. Every member is going to identify the biggest leak in their company and get placed into the right implementation path.

This is how we make the Circle more useful, more practical, and more valuable.
```

## Day 8: Have Current Members Take The Audit

Do not guess what they need.

Diagnose.

## Day 10: Run The First Reset Call

Theme:

```text
Where contractors leak profit and how Contractor Circle is going to fix it.
```

## Day 11: Turn The Call Into Content

Create:

- 3 reels.
- 1 email.

## Day 12: Publish The First Lead Magnet Campaign

Post every day around the Profit Leak Audit.

## Day 13: Promote Contractor Circle Directly

Use the positioning:

```text

```

## Day 14: Review Numbers

Track:

- Audit opt-ins.
- Email replies.
- Circle interest.
- Current member participation.

- Tool usage.
- Member stuck points.
