# Content Machine

## Purpose

Build celebrity, expose pain, teach the mechanism, and create demand for Contractor Circle.

Content should not be random.

Every piece should follow the same strategic job:

```text
Expose the leak.
Show why it costs money.
Teach the simple system principle.
Point to the audit, the tool, or Contractor Circle.
```

## Six Content Pillars

### 1. Profit Leaks

Purpose:

- Main public hook.

Examples:

```text
You are not broke because you need more leads.

You are broke because money is leaking after you win the job.
```

```text
Most contractors do not know their profit is gone until the job is already over.
```

```text
Revenue hides leaks. Profit exposes them.
```

CTA:

- Contractor Profit Leak Audit.

### 2. Estimating

Purpose:

- Capture contractors who think they need more or better work.

Examples:

```text
The estimate is where the job starts making money or losing money.
```

```text
If your estimate does not define assumptions, exclusions, and risk, your field team will pay for it later.
```

```text
Stop estimating to win. Start estimating to protect profit.
```

CTA:

- Estimating Profit Checklist.
- Contractor Profit Leak Audit.
- Contractor Circle.

### 3. Change Orders

Purpose:

- Easiest pain to sell.

Examples:

```text
A change order is not money until it is approved, billed, and collected.
```

```text
If your change orders live in text messages, you are not tracking money. You are hoping.
```

```text
Extra work without a weekly review process becomes free work.
```

CTA:

- Change Order Cash Tracker.
- Contractor Circle.

### 4. Project Profit Control

Purpose:

- Bridge into IOR and OverWatch.

Examples:

```text
Your PM should not just know if the job is on schedule. He should know where the money is.
```

```text
A job can look fine in a status meeting and still be bleeding profit.
```

```text
Project management without financial control is just organized guessing.
```

CTA:

- Project Profit Control Sprint.
- Contractor Circle.

### 5. Owner Bottleneck

Purpose:

- Bridge into AOS.

Examples:

```text
Your company is not disorganized because your people are bad. It is disorganized because you are still the operating system.
```

```text
If every important decision comes through you, you do not have a business. You have a dependency machine.
```

```text
The owner should not be the meeting, the scorecard, the issue list, and the accountability system.
```

- Contractor Circle.

## Basic Content Formula

```text
Pain hook
Specific contractor problem
Why it is costing money
Simple system principle
Private tool / Circle CTA
```

Example:

```text
Most contractors are not losing money because they are bad at building.

They are losing money because extra work is happening in the field, getting talked about in texts, and never making it cleanly into approval, billing, and collection.

That is not a hustle problem. That is a system problem.

Every active job should have a weekly change-order cash review.

Inside Contractor Circle, this is one of the first tools we install because it turns hidden exposure into visible money.
```
