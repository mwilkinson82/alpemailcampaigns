# Landing Page Requirements

Landing pages should be built after the email and tracking infrastructure is defined.

Before publishing any landing page, confirm:

- The form creates or updates the contact.
- The contact is added to the correct audience.
- The lead magnet value is saved.
- The source and UTM values are saved.
- The audit scores are saved if the form is the audit.
- The correct Resend automation starts.
- The Day 0 or delivery email sends.
- The unsubscribe path works.
- The event appears in the dashboard.

## Access Rule

Public pages can show:

- Diagnostic language.
- Lite tools.
- Blurred or sample screenshots.
- Tool previews.
- Call clips.
- Member win summaries.
- Contractor Circle invitation.

Public pages should not expose:

- Full Hub tools.
- Full implementation tracks.
- Assignments.
- Call replay libraries.
- Member wins board.
- AOS, IOR, or OverWatch full systems.
